Guest Speaker: Rose Bender

Response
Epiphanies/Insights: Rose ended up sending upwards of 100 resumes out with very few reaching back out. As a graduating senior, I think that was important for me to hear, the harsh reality of job searching.

Reflection: I think Rose's persistence in finding a job in a field that she would enjoy is really important. She did projects that she would learn from but really wanted to find something she would enjoy. Even though I am graduating with a specialty in graphic design, I really enjoy working where I am now and will keep doing it until I want to do something new.

Three Key Points:
- Make good connections, both in real life and online. Rose's good relationship with the hiring manager is one of the key points of her being able to go back and land a job at a company she was rejected from before.
- Don't be afraid to put yourself out there. Send out as many resumes as you can (and cover letters help) so that people will at least have you in their system
- Don't give up!! It may be tiring sending out so many resumes but that's how you'll make connections and get experience.

Disagreements/Differences: N/A

Prior Knowledge: Job searching is hard and you really have to just put your name out there.
